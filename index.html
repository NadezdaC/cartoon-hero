About Babu Frik

Babu Frik is a tiny and efficient alien, capable of modifying nearly any droid.

Babu Frik at work

Babu Frik's Characteristics

  1. He can reprogram or modify almost any droid, regardless of its security measures
  2. He speaks a heavily accented form of Basic known as Anzellan
  3. He can bypass C-3PO's programming restrictions
